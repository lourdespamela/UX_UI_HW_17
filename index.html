Project Tour

Nonprofit Organization Responsive Web Redesign

My group and I conducted user interviews where we learned about our user's emotions that motivate them to want to donate to a non-profit organization and about their navigation habits and preferences. Through these interviews and 5-second usability testing, we discovered that FillaHeart4Kids' website lacks information and accessibility that users would want to experience in order to donate to the organization. My group and I worked to address these issues by redesigning the website and iterating as needed.

Nesters Mobile App

Many mothers suffer from perinatal mental health symptoms and are often not asked about their mental wellbeing or struggle to ask for help. Nesters is an app that offers many features primarily for mothers to track their mood and symptoms, playfully communicate to others how they feel, and suggest ideas to the mother’s family and friends on how to support them.

Government Agency Responsive Web Redesign

The Department of Homeland Security’s (DHS) navigation system is not helpful for predicting what information to expect on web pages within the site and has an overwhelming amount of information on the homepage. I redesigned the homepage and navigation systems to make the accurate information more accessible to users.
