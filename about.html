About Me

I’m a first-generation college student always looking to do work that makes the people around me proud. I graduated in June 2020 with a B.A. in Political Science with a double minor in Psychology and Sociology. A year later being the curious learner that I am I enrolled in a UX/UI design bootcamp.

When I first started my journey as a UX designer, I quickly learned that this was going to be the field that I love enough to breakaway from my comfort zone. Growing up, I shied away from creativity because I wanted to make sure that everything I did was being done correctly. The only way I was programmed to measure success was by completing a set or rules or instructions exactly.

My experiences in UX design showed me the importance of embracing creativity and challenging myself when designing solutions for user problems. Now, I don’t measure success by perfection, I measure it by my ability to learn from user reseach and usability tests to design solutions.
